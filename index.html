What is BMI?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. The formula is BMI = kg/m² where kg is a person's weight in kilograms and m² is their height in meters squared. It can also be calculated using pounds and inches by using the formula BMI = (weight in pounds / (height in inches x height in inches)) x 703.
